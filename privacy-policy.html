SpeakEase AAC — Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Overview

SpeakEase AAC ("the App") is an augmentative and alternative communication (AAC) application developed by Sentiq Robotics, designed for children and individuals with communication needs. This privacy policy explains how the App handles data. We are committed to protecting the privacy of all users, especially children under 13, in compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, transmit, or share personal information for advertising, analytics, or tracking. Specifically:

  • No advertising IDs or device fingerprinting
  • No location data collection
  • No contact list or phone number access
  • No third-party analytics or tracking SDKs
  • No social media integration

Information Stored Locally

The App stores the following data on your device only:

  • User profiles (display name and preferences)
  • Vocabulary customizations and custom words
  • Word usage statistics (for prediction improvement)
  • Sentence history
  • Generated stories (saved to Story Library)
  • Custom audio recordings
  • App preferences (theme, voice, grid layout)
  • PIN hash for parental access control

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly use Cloud Backup or an optional AI feature.

Text-to-Speech

The App uses the device's built-in text-to-speech engine to speak words and sentences aloud. All speech processing happens on-device. No audio or text is sent to external servers for TTS.

Optional AI Features (Parent-Gated)

The following features require a parent/caregiver to provide their own OpenAI API key in settings. They are disabled by default and gated behind parental controls:

  • Daily Story Generator — sends today's communicated words (not names or PII) to OpenAI to generate a bedtime story. Limited to one story per day.
  • AI Sentence Completion — sends partial sentences to OpenAI for completion suggestions.
  • AI Image Generation — sends a word label to OpenAI to create a vocabulary image.
  • AI Communication Coach — sends anonymized word usage patterns (no names) to OpenAI for coaching tips.

These features use "the child" or "our friend" instead of real names to protect privacy. The API key is stored encrypted (AES-256) on-device.

OpenAI's data usage policy applies to these requests: https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy

On-Device Translation

The App uses Google ML Kit for on-device language translation. Translation models are downloaded to the device and all translation happens locally. No text is sent to Google servers for translation.

Cloud Backup (Optional)

Parents/caregivers may enable Google Drive cloud backup:

  • Requires Google Sign-In (email used for authentication only)
  • Backup data is encrypted with AES-256-CBC before upload
  • Stored in Google Drive's hidden app folder (not visible in Drive)
  • Only the last 3 backups are kept; older ones are automatically deleted
  • Can be disabled at any time in settings

Google's privacy policy applies to data stored on Google Drive: https://policies.google.com/privacy

In-App Purchases

The App uses RevenueCat to manage optional premium subscriptions and one-time purchases. RevenueCat processes:

  • Anonymous user ID (no name or email)
  • Purchase receipts from Google Play
  • Subscription status

No personal information is shared with RevenueCat. Their privacy policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

Camera & NFC

The App may request camera access for QR code scanning and NFC access for vocabulary tags. These features:

  • Process data entirely on-device
  • Do not transmit images or NFC data externally
  • Are optional and can be denied at the system level

Audio Recording

The App allows parents/caregivers to record custom audio clips for vocabulary words. Recordings are stored locally on the device and are never transmitted externally.

Parental Controls

The App includes PIN-protected parental controls. Settings that modify the app's behavior, manage data, enable AI features, or access purchases require parent/caregiver verification through a PIN code or math challenge. The PIN is stored as a PBKDF2-HMAC-SHA256 hash and cannot be recovered from stored data.

Data Deletion

All app data can be deleted at any time by:

  • Deleting a user profile (removes all associated data)
  • Signing out of Google (removes cloud backup access)
  • Clearing the app's data through device settings
  • Uninstalling the App

Deletion of local data is immediate and permanent. Cloud backups on Google Drive can be removed from the backup settings screen.

COPPA Compliance

This App complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):

  • Parental consent is required before first use
  • No personal information is collected from children
  • AI features are disabled by default and parent-gated
  • No names or PII are sent to external APIs
  • Parents can review and delete all data at any time
  • No advertising or behavioral tracking

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above and will be available within the App and on this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
